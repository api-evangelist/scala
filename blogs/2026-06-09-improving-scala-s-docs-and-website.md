This post covers work done under the Sovereign Tech Fund investment . The work is coordinated by the Scala Center . We have collected possible documentation tasks from three sources: Tasks in the original proposal we made to the Sovereign Tech Agency Tasks collected in internal discussions since then Tasks from community suggestions on this Contributors forum thread This blog post summarizes what we have collected, identifies tasks that we have chosen to tackle, and describes work that is already underway.
