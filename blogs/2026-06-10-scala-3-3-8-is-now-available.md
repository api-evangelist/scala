Scala 3.3.8 LTS is now available! This patch release backports most of the bugfixes and some of the improvements introduced in the Scala Next series to the Scala 3.8.4 release. All of the backported changes were proven to not break either binary or source compatibility, by testing over 1500 projects in the Scala 3 Open Community Build .
